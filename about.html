About Me

This is what I do.

I am Anna Heckadon. I am majoring in Anthropology at the University of Victoria in BC, Canada. My focus is on Cultural Anthropology and Archaeology. This blog is made in conjuntion with my Anthropology class on Digital Archaeology (Anth 485).

What I've Done

In Summer 2017, I got the opportunity to intern at the Mendocino County Museum in Willits, California where I helped them get ready for their new exhibit "Main Street: Then and Now".

In the latter half of my summer I interned at Byggðasafn Reykjanesbæjar, a museum in Iceland, where I collected data on the condition of their storage area.
